Self-Care Advice
Stay Calm

Try to stay calm. Anxiety can make symptoms feel worse, so focus on your breathing. Take deep breaths and try to relax your mind. If you feel light-headed or dizzy, sit down and rest.

Stay Hydrated

Sip water regularly. Staying hydrated is important to help your body cope. Avoid drinking too much at once. Small sips will prevent discomfort and help hydrate you better.

Find a Comfortable Position

Sit upright if you feel breathless, and avoid lying down to help your breathing. Try sitting with your back straight and leaning slightly forward if possible. It will make breathing easier.
